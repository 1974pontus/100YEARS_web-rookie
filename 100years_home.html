100 YEARS

A 100 years is about how long we humans can grasp the time given to us on this planet. There are people that live for longer than that and can actually remember what happend 100 years ago. think about that...

The Swedish indie-metal act 100 YEARS was formed back in 2012. The members are all regular forty-something guys with a slightly extraordinary background in music; ranging from death metal pioneering in the ground breaking band Merciless to Swedish household punk rock band Dia Psalma. What brought these random pieces together was the soft spot for mood as well as noise.
When you accidentally hit that nerve, that open wound, that resonates with your actual bones. That is the monment we are constantly searching for. And when you can transcend that thing to the listener you are on to something huge. Lyric-wise the subjects describes the darkness, loneliness and emptiness that evolves around us, the humans. The music itself can be boiled down to three words: pretention, soul and monotony. Voivod meets Swans, or Killing Joke meets Celtic Frost – well, let your ears decide.

SUICIDE RECORDS

The finest recordlabel that ever came out of this god forsaken cold part of the world. Tons of excellent releases from them. I all started with the love for extreme and ugly music. Two great friends Roger and Henrik took things in their own hands created the label. Now it has grown and even has an office in Finland that also releases strange and dark noises from the finnish underground. Noise and thunder and always with an artistic edge to all their signed acts. You should really check them out. It´s an order.
